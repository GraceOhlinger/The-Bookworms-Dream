Meditation

By Tara Ward

$10.00

Wouldn't it be wonderful to meet all the stresses of modern life with calm and compsure. This insightful guide shows you how to meditate in a variety of ways that appeal to all of the senses. From quick mini-meditations to learning how to use scent and sound to transport yourself into another state of mind, there is plenty to try for those seeking daily peace and harmony.

Learn how to work with chakras and energy to clear your mind and access deep meditation, with all the benefits that it imparts. You can walk the path to serenity and enjoy blissful, mind-expanding experiences.
