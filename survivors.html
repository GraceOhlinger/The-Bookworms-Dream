The Survivors

By Jane Harper

$10.00

Do not purchase any of these books if you are not comfortable with the following topics: murder, disappearances/kidnapping


Kieran Elliot's life changed forever on the day a reckless mistake led to devastating consequences.

The guilt that still haunts him resurfaces during a visit with his young family to the small coastal community he once called home.

Kieran's parents are struggling in a town where fourtunes are forged by the sea. Between them all is his absent brother, Finn.

When a body is discovered on the beach, long-held secrets threaten to emerge. A sunken wreck, a missing girl, and questions that have never washed away...
