Blink

by Malcolm Gladwell

$7.75

Drawing on cutting-edge neuroscience and psychology to reveal that the difference between good decision making and bad has less to do with how much information we process than with our ability to focus on a few particular details, Gladwell shows how we can all become better decision makers---in our homes, in our offices, and in everyday life.
