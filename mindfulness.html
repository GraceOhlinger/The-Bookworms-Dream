Mindfulness

By Wendy Hobson

Learn how reconnecting with your body in the present moment can yield enormous benefits in mental and physical well-being. Mindful meditation can enable you to cope with the ups and downs of daily life, while ensuring that you maintain a core of unwavering calm.
