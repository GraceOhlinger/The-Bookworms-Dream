Milk and Honey is a collection of poetry about love, loss, trauma, abuse, healing, and femininity. It is split into four chapters. Each chapter serves as a different purpose, deals with a different pain, deals with a different heartache. Milk and Honey takes readers through a journey of the most bitter moments in life...see more
