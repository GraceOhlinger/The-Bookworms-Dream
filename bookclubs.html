Book Clubs Galore!

One way to get outside of your comfort zone is trying something new. A great example for people who love books is joining a book club! Book clubs are an awesome way to meet new people, make new friends, and to obviously talk about books! On this page, you will find resources on how to start your own book club as well as how to create an account on our website when starting your book club. Visit our contact page and fill out our form if you have any questions or concerns.




How to Start Your Own Book Club

Find Out What You Are Interested In:

This may take a bit of time if you are unsure of what exactly you are interested in, and that's totally ok! This is the time to discover something new and exciting! A good place to start is browsing though our reccomendations on our bookstore. We offer titles from genres like fiction, fantasy, sci-fi, and so much more. If there is something you can't seem to find on our bookstore, your local public library is another great resource in finding exactly what you are looking for.


Create An Account To Get Started For Your Book Club:

Through our website, we also have a portal available where you can see who might have the same interests as you! To create an account, simply follow these instructions:

  1. Go to our Login link, which you can find on the top right of your screen.

  2. At the bottom of our login page, click on create a new account. From there, you will be able to fill out a form asking you for some information like a username and password that you will use to login to your account.

  3. After you create your account, you are then able to find others that have the same interests as you, whether it would be genre, author, a particular book, etc.

  4. Once you are able to find the appropriate amount of people for your bookclub, you and your group can decide on book club meetings by messaging one another though the portal.

Note: We also offer a list of questions to consider when starting your meetings which you can find here. Good luck on your new journey and Happy Reading!
