Book Clubs

The Famous Detectives

Are you a fan of mysteries and thrillers? If so, you have come to the right place! We meet at the end of every month at 6:00pm EST. The location is announced a week before each meeting via email.




Gandalf's Apprentices

Have you ever wondered what it would be like to discover a world unlike our own? Are you a fan of The Lord of the Rings, or perhaps Harry Potter? Then join us at our book club today! We meet on the 1st of every other month at 4:00pm PST on Zoom. We provide the link to you via email during the last week of the month previous prior to our meetings.




The Rise of Pluto

What makes sci-fi really sci-fi to you? Is it the UFOs? The aliens? The future? You can find out through our sci-fi bookclub! We meet the second week of each month at 2:00pm EST. We provide the location of each meeting one week prior via email and text.
