About Us

The Bookworm's Dream was founded in 2024 with the intent to encourage people to read. We want to be able to have everyone become aware of the joys of picking up a good book, and discovering a new world different from ours. Here you will find ways to not only expand your own collection of books, you can also figure out how to join, and even create, your own bookclub.


Our Mission

To encourage younger generations by reading more, connecting with others through the power of bookclubs, and to be able to add new books to evergrowing collections for a reasonable price.


Our Goals

  1. Giving the best experience for our users/customers

  2. Encouraging others to read more

  3. To offer reasonable prices on books, along with offering discounts to ensure that everyone will be able to discover their next favorite read

  4. To give our users the opportunity to create new friendships through a shared hobby without the fear of being judged
